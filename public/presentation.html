A Silent, Dangerous Problem

125,000+

Deaths per year caused by medication non-adherence & drug interactions (Annals of Internal Medicine)

70%

Of Indian families managing 2+ chronic conditions simultaneously across generations

💡 Key Insight: When grandfather takes Warfarin and grandson adds Aspirin — nobody warns them. That combination can cause fatal bleeding.

SwaasthyaGraph Solution

🔗 Family Health Graph
Models patients, drugs & symptoms as connected nodes in a live Neo4j AuraDB database.
💊 Drug Conflict Detection
Graph traversal instantly flags dangerous interactions (e.g. Aspirin + Warfarin = ⚠️ CONFLICT).
🗣️ Multilingual Voice Logging
Speak in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu — Sarvam AI transcribes, translates & extracts health entities automatically.
⚙️ Durable Health Workflows
Render Workflows runs background pipelines: scan → detect → summarise → alert.
